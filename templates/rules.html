📖 Rules & Regulations

Please follow these guidelines to ensure a fair, secure, and enjoyable library experience for all users.

📅 Borrowing Period

All borrowed books must be returned on or before the assigned due date.

📚 Borrowing Limit

Each user may borrow a maximum of 3 books at any one time.

⏰ Late Returns

Repeated late returns may result in penalties, borrowing restrictions, or temporary suspension.

💻 Personal Use Only

E-books and digital resources are provided strictly for personal educational use.

🔒 Copyright Protection

Copying, sharing, reproducing, or distributing digital materials without permission is prohibited.

🛡️ Website Security

Any attempt to hack, manipulate, damage, or disrupt the system is strictly prohibited.

👤 Privacy & Data

Personal information is protected and used solely for library management purposes.

⚠️ Violations

Users who violate these rules may receive warnings, account suspension, or permanent account removal.

Important Notice

By using EasyLibrary, users agree to follow all rules and regulations. These guidelines help maintain a safe, organized, and accessible library environment for everyone.
