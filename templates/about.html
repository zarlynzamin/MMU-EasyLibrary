📚 About EasyLibrary

Your Digital Gateway to Knowledge

Our Story

EasyLibrary was developed to provide a simple and convenient way for users to access library resources online. Traditional library systems often require users to search for books manually and manage borrowing records through physical processes.

To improve this experience, EasyLibrary offers a user-friendly digital platform where users can browse categories, borrow books, track their borrowed items, and manage their profiles with ease.

Our goal is to encourage reading and make knowledge more accessible by providing a modern and efficient library management system.

How to Use EasyLibrary

1 Browse book categories on the Home page.
2 Select a category and explore available books.
3 Click the Borrow button to borrow a book.
4 Visit My Books to view your borrowed books.
5 Manage your information through the Profile page.
6 Follow the Rules & Regulations for borrowing policies.
