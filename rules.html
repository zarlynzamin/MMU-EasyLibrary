Rules and Regulations

1. Borrowed books must be returned before the due date given.

2. Users may borrow only 3 books per person at a time.

3. Late returns may result in penalties or temporary suspension.

4. Borrowed e-books or materials are for personal use only.

5. Copying, sharing, or distributing digital books without permission is prohibited.

6. Attempting to hack, damage, or disrupt the website is strictly prohibited.

7. Personal information will be protected and used only for website operations.

8. Violating any rules may lead to warnings, suspension or permanent account removal.
